About My Journey as Dancer

Hello, I am Tejaswi, a diligent kuchipudi dancer. I have been learning Kuchipudi since my UKG. It all started with my mom having interest to learn during her childhood but failed to do so due to lack of facilties. So she started sending me to Kuhcipudi classes when I was a tiny tot. Gradually , the interest , the love, the passion to dance chased me every minute.
